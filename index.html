FITNESS EDGE

At Fitness Edge, we provide what the sport cannot. We offer programs for everyone of all ages, gender, and skill level. Fitness Edge is the premier facility in the Santa Clarita Valley to help reach your fitness, weight, or athletic goals. Our class focuses on small group training, so that you receive the attention you need and deserve. This type of training will maximize results, ensure proper technique, and limit injuries.

Unlike other gyms or boxes, our coaches are not only certified, but also hold college degrees. All of them have completed, and excelled, at high level sports and athletics. They are required to attend seminars and continue education, in our field, to make sure that we stay on the cutting edge of training. Come in for a free trial today.

ELEMENTARY

During this phase in life, children are like sponges. They thirst for knowledge and are hungry to develop new skills. These classes are 60 minutes long and are programmed to build a foundationof proper movement patterns, running and jumping technique, balance, stabilization, strength, and overall athletic coordination. We strive to build and encourage our athletes' self esteem, as well as improve performance, helping to prevent injury in a fun and positive atmosphere.
